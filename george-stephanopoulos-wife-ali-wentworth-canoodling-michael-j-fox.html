George and Ali have shared some details about their relationship that fans wished they would have just kept quiet.

"Uh, George Stephanopoulos snores," she said.

"He does?" Ryan asked.

"....Like a drunk old truck driver," Ali finished.

The crowd laughed and Ryan asked what she did to stop it.

"First, I have to breathe because I'm so full of rage. And if it's really bad, I push him, I turn him," Ali said.

"And if he doesn't stop, I go down and sleep in my older daughter's room."

"You do?" Ryan asked.

"Yeah. Then, he comes down and wakes me up and makes me go back to bed," Ali said through gritted teeth.

"With a job like this or that, you have to get sleep," Ryan said.

"Yes!" Ali responded.

After their discussion, Ryan read tips for stopping snoring including drinking more water, taking baths before bed, and drinking less alcohol before going to sleep.

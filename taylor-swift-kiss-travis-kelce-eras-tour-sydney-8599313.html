Watch Taylor Swift Kiss Travis Kelce at Eras Tour in Sydney

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Taylor Swift's The Eras Tour touched down in Sydney on Friday and delivered a wealth of events along with its arrival to the city down under. There were more than a few celebrities in attendance, some inclement weather to dial up the drama, and romance galore as the pop star's boyfriend — Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce — was her final stop before leaving the stadium.

After singing her heart out to fans, the "Anti-Hero" singer gave Swifties a treat by leaving through the stadium doors, where Kelce patiently waited for her on the sidelines. The pop star waved out to those remaining in the Accor Stadium and then quickly walked up to the football star, planting a kiss on him before walking out together arm in arm.

Love was in the air throughout the night, as Swift gave Kelce a shoutout not once, but twice — while she performed "Willow" from 2020's Evermore, several fans speculated that she pointed at him while singing the lyrics "That's my man." Swift changed the lyrics to Midnights track, "Karma," too — “Karma is the guy on the Chiefs, coming straight home to me,” she sang, a call back to when she pulled out the same lyric change during her Argentina tour stop in November.

The kiss may offer up a bit of déjà vu for for fans, as Swift also gave Kelce a major kiss on the lips (she literally ran into his arms) after the Argentina concert.

Night one of The Eras Tour stop in Sydney also saw Katy Perry supporting Swift, even singing along to "Bad Blood." Kelce was also gifted several friendship bracelets from fans, and Swift whipped out "How You Get the Girl" and "White Horse" (a duet with Sabrina Carpenter) as her surprise songs for the night. "Long Live" The Eras Tour and its many updates.

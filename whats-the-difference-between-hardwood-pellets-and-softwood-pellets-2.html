Wood pellets are the most common type of pellet fuel and are generally made from compacted sawdust and related industrial wastes from the milling of lumber, manufacture of wood products and furniture, and construction. They can be used as fuels for power generation, commercial or residential heating, and cooking. Just so, can you mix hardwood and softwood pellets?

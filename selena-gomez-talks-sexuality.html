Selena Gomez Admits She's Questioned Her Sexuality, Plus More News!

June 2024 · 2 minute read

- One year after photos surfaced of Selena Gomez and Cara Delevingne bathing together, Gomez admits she "loved" the rumors surrounding her sexuality. When asked if she's ever questioned her own, the "Good For You" singer said, " I think everybody does, no matter who they are...I think it's healthy to gain a perspective on who you are deep down, question yourself and challenge yourself; it's important to do that." E! News

- In an appearance on Watch What Happens Live, Taylor Kinney reminisced about the first time he and Lady Gaga locked lips, saying she slapped him during the filming of the "You and I" music video. The couple got engaged in February after dating for four years.  US Weekly

- Actress Cate Blanchett said she believes complex female film roles should be the norm, and no longer be considered a "breakthrough" for women. "I think there's a swathe of great roles for women and certainly, there's some wonderful female performers. It's just time to get on with it really," the Carol actress said at the BFI London Film Festival. E! News

- Bill Murray gathered Hollywood's best for his Netflix original A Very Murray Christmas, available this December on the streaming site. Celebrities such as Miley Cyrus, Jenny Lewis, Amy Poehler and Chris Rock are just a few who make an appearance. E!

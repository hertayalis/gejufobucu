Tia &amp; Tamera Mowry Go to Disneyland to Celebrate Son Cree's Birthday

September 2024 · 2 minute read

American actress, Tia Dashon Mowry-Hardrict, celebrates her son, Cree Taylor Hardrict's 7th birthday during a family vacation to Disneyland.

After dating for six years, German-born, American actress Tia Mowry and American actor Cory Hardrict got engaged on the 25th of December 2006 and got married on the 20th of April 2008.

The couple had their first child, son, Cree Taylor Hardrict on the 28th of June 2011. On the 5th of May 2018, Cree Hardrict became a big brother as the couple welcomed their second child and daughter, Cairo Tiahna Hardrict.

Although Cree's birthday is still a few days away, the Mowry family, however, chose to celebrate his new age as the couple, Tia Mowry and Cory Hardrict were accompanied by some other family members on a trip to Disneyland.

Cree received special treatment from his family member; the celebration was opened with a birthday song and good wishes as he held a cupcake with a candle on it in his hands. The adorable boy wore a massive smile as love was showered on him by his family and relatives.

The family enjoyed a thrilling time in Disneyland, trying out different rides and fun activities. The memory of this trip will forever be in the minds of the family members.

Although some may argue that Cree takes after his father, Cory Hardrict, because Cree is fast growing and tall just like his Hardrict, Tia is confident that her son takes after her and her daughter takes after her husband.

"Thanks for just being HERE. Ps, whenever you take a photo, of course, everyone says SMILE, and as you can see, #Cree is definitely my son, and #cairo is definitely @coryhardrict daughter!”

Aside from being able to perform different facial expressions like his mother, Cree also has a love for traveling just like Tia.

Tia once stated that she and her son share a love for traveling. She said,

“I love to travel.” “Cree also loves to travel,” the celebrity mom said, “My hope and my dream is that he will continue to want to travel with his mommy when he gets older.”

From the images shared online, the trip which had Tamera Mowry, her husband Adam Housley, their kids and uncle Tahj Mowry in attendance, thus look like it was quite an excellent one.
